Every Roofing Service
Done Right

Residential and commercial roofing in Riverside, CA — start to finish.

New Build & Construction

New Roof Installation

A correctly installed roof is your property's most important investment. We work with all roofing materials — shingles, tile, flat, and metal — and build every installation to California code and beyond.

We'll help you choose the right system for your climate, structure, and budget, then execute it with precision.

  • Full material selection consultation
  • Residential and commercial installs
  • All roof types: shingle, tile, flat, metal
  • Built to California code standards
  • Clean worksite, professional crew
Full Replacement

Roof Replacement

When your current roof has reached end of life, a full replacement restores protection, curb appeal, and property value. We tear off the old system, inspect the decking, and build from the ground up — properly.

We'll be upfront about whether replacement is actually necessary. No unnecessary upsells — ever.

  • Complete tear-off and decking inspection
  • Honest assessment — we only replace what's needed
  • Material options to match your home's style
  • Fast project completion
  • Full cleanup included
Leaks & Damage

Roof Repair

A small problem can become a big one fast. Whether it's a leak, cracked tiles, missing shingles, flashing failure, or storm damage — we diagnose accurately and fix it right. No inflated scopes.

Most repairs are completed in a single visit. We'll tell you the cost before we start.

  • Accurate leak detection and diagnosis
  • Storm and wind damage repair
  • Flashing, shingle, and tile repair
  • Same-day service available
  • Transparent pricing before work begins
Peace of Mind

Roof Inspection

You can't protect what you can't see. A professional inspection identifies damage, wear, and vulnerabilities before they become expensive. Ideal before purchasing a property, after severe weather, or as routine annual maintenance.

  • Pre-purchase and real estate inspections
  • Post-storm assessments
  • Annual condition checks
  • Detailed written report provided
  • No-pressure follow-up recommendations
Cost-Effective Upgrade

Reroofing

When the underlying structure is sound but the surface is worn, reroofing is the smart, cost-effective solution. We install a new layer over existing material — saving time and money compared to a full tear-off.

  • Saves time and cost vs. full replacement
  • Available for qualifying roof types
  • Honest structural assessment included
  • New surface life and improved weatherproofing
  • Professional clean finish
🔄
Maintenance

Roof Cleaning

Moss, algae, lichen, and debris don't just look bad — they actively degrade your roofing materials. Professional cleaning removes harmful growth, prevents moisture retention, and restores your roof's appearance.

  • Safe removal of moss, algae, and lichen
  • Debris and gutter-line clearing
  • Extends roof lifespan significantly
  • Improves curb appeal and home value
  • Safe techniques that won't damage materials
